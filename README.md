# hrcm-website

This repo contains the Jekyll codebase for the HRCM website ([**hrcm.ceda.ac.uk**](https://hrcm/ceda.ac.uk)).

The High-Resolution Climate Modelling (HRCM) group is a long-standing collaboration between scientists from two UK institutions at the frontier of exploiting weather-resolving models to simulate the building blocks of Earth's climate—the National Centre for Atmospheric Science (University of Reading) and Met Office Hadley Centre for Climate Science and Services.
